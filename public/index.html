Hello there! My name is Ziyang Zhang, currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Informatics at Emory University, advised by Dr. Carl Yang. My research interests are in multimodal graph learning and interpretable AI for health. I am also a researcher in National Institutes of Health (NIH)’s All of Us Research Program. Before my Ph.D. journey, I was pursuing a Master’s degree at Emory and exited the program in 2024 when I transferred to the Ph.D. I obtained my Bachelor’s degree at Shenzhen University.

Outside of my research, I enjoy what the life offers me. I play video games and watch anime & TV shows, where I love Attack on Titan most (may peace prevail). The hobbies from virtual world are more like a private haven for every night where I recharge my spirits and then engage more fully in workout, soccer (actually prefer to call it football), boxing, hiking, and so on. If you’ve been to my parties, you also know that I’m a (amateur) bartender and barista. In the coming weeks, I hope to explore the following interests: French cuisine cooking, quantitative trading, and inscriptions on the BTC and ETH blockchains.
